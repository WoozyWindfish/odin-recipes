Creme Brulee Pan

This is a fairly easy desert to make, so long as you are comfortable with frying. These rolls are best made with a cream filling roll. This simple two ingredient desert makes for a simple, yet decidant desert.

Ingredients

  • Water x 1 Tablespoon
  • Sugar x 2 Tablespoons
  • Cream-Filled bread rolls (marupan)
  • Canola Oil (or oil of your choice)

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a pan until it reaches about 180 degrees.
  2. Add the cream-filled bread rolls and fry each side for about one minute or until golden brown.
  3. Remove the rolls and set them aside to cool down.
  4. In a separate pot, mix the water and sugar. Heat until the mixture comes to a boil.
  5. Once the boiling sugar reaches a darker brown color, remove from heat and pour the mixture over the rolls.
  6. Once the rolls are done cooling (about 20-30 minutes) enjoy!
